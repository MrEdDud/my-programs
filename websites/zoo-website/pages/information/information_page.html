Information about the zoo

Immerse yourself in the beauty and diversity of the animal kingdom as you explore our meticulously designed animal exhibits. From the lush rainforests of South America to the vast savannas of Africa, each exhibit offers a unique glimpse into the natural habitats of our resident species. Get up close and personal with exotic animals, including lions, tigers, elephants, and more, while learning about their behaviors, adaptations, and conservation status.

Take home a piece of your zoo experience with a visit to our Gift Shop, where you'll find a wide selection of souvenirs, gifts, and keepsakes to commemorate your visit. Browse through a diverse range of merchandise, including plush toys, apparel, home decor, and eco-friendly products inspired by our resident animals and conservation efforts. Every purchase supports our mission of wildlife conservation and habitat preservation.

Information about our facilities

At Riget Zoo Adventures, our mission is simple yet profound: to inspire curiosity, foster appreciation, and promote conservation of wildlife and their habitats. Guided by our core values of education, conservation, and community engagement, we are dedicated to creating meaningful experiences that connect people with nature and empower them to make a positive impact on the planet.

Whether you're a family seeking adventure, a wildlife enthusiast, or a curious explorer, Riget Zoo Adventures offers something for everyone. Enjoy a leisurely stroll through our lush botanical gardens, embark on a safari adventure aboard our train or tram, or treat your taste buds to delicious dining options and refreshing beverages available throughout the zoo.
